You know that saying, “love at first sight?” That wasn’t me and design. It took a few tries — and a few years — before I realized how much I genuinely enjoyed it. I grew up loving video games, but after realizing how much work goes into making one, I pivoted to something slightly more manageable: designing websites and apps. Fortunately, I found those to be way more interesting instead.

I was born and raised in Jakarta, Indonesia, and I’m currently studying at Simon Fraser University’s School of Interactive Arts & Technology on a full-ride scholarship. I specialize in UI/UX design and interaction design, with proficiency in tools like Figma, Adobe Creative Suite, HTML/CSS, and Protopie. I love learning new tools and jumping into unfamiliar workflows for the thrill of the game. Outside of design, I enjoy cooking, sewing, and occasionally falling victim to well-targeted online ads.
